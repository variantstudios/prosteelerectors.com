Welcome to Pro Steel Erectors, Inc.

Thank you for visiting our website.  Pro Steel was founded in 1999 and is headquartered in Kingsbury, TX.  We specialize in commercial and industrial steel erection projects.  Our workforce and management team operates and is licensed in: Texas, Arizona, California, New Mexico, Utah, and Nevada.  We also pursue government institutions and military bases nationwide.    

Here at Pro Steel we take great pride in the work we perform and enjoy being a team player.  Quality and performance of our work is second only to safety.  Our workmen are equipped with the proper tools and necessary equipment needed to stay 100 percent tied off, while working above six feet, and safely erect steel structures.  Working with our management staff, an erection plan is composed by a licensed structural engineer, complete with critical lift analysis and temporary guying, shoring and bracing plans.  Erection plans are followed closely and enforced by our safety, supervision, and quality control departments.

Thank you again for visiting our site, please notice examples of our up front planning and portfolio of completed and current projects. 

Please feel free to contact our sales department to begin working with our team.  Bid documents may be uploaded to this site by our clients and prospective clients.
