McCarthy

Dear Mr. Michaels,

We at McCarthy Building Companies, Twin Rivers Project would like to acknowledge the achievements Pro Steel Erectors Inc. (Pro Steel) has made in regards to their safety program implementation.

We understand the difficulty in implementing an effective safety program in a High Hazard Industry with constantly changing challenges. Pro Steel Erectors Inc. has allocated the necessary to recourses, shown a genuine concern and demonstrated commitment to the safety and well being of their workers.

Policies, procedures, implementation and enforcement of those policies are critical to the safety of all involved. Pro Steel, through the efforts of their leadership and crew, has demonstrated their commitment to an effective safety program, which includes a strong enforcement element.

McCarthy Building Company sincerely appreciates this worthy effort.

Respectfully,
McCarthy Building Companies, Inc.

Scott Leiby
Safety Manager
